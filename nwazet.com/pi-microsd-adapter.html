Adafruit MicroSD Card Adapter For Raspberry Pi

$5.95

Product Type: Cables & Connectors

With this microSD card adapter, you can help protect your Raspberry Pi from accidental damage to the SD card port and from file system corruptions caused by knocking the protruding SD card.

The adapter snaps into the Raspberry Pi's SD card slot, but only takes the space of half a card. Insert a micro-SD into it, and get a more compact and safer system. The micro-SD is easily extracted by pushing on it a second time.

Please be aware that this adapter will not fit into most Raspberry Pi cases.

Specifications

  • Compatible with any micro-SD card
  • Protrudes 3mm from the edge of the Raspberry Pi
  • 5mm thick (clearance from the bottom of the Pi's PCB)
  • A micro-SD card is not included

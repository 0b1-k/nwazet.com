Nwazet Pi Minitux Case (Clear)

$9.00

Product Type: Cases & Plates

We built the Minitux for our customers who want the elegance of our larger Tuxedo case but need a smaller format that remains as close as possible to the Pi board.

This beautiful laser-cut clear acrylic case is designed to give a close fit to a Raspberry Pi model A or B while giving easy access to all of the Pi's connectors.

The clear acrylic it's made of lets you see your Pi in all its glory at all times. Generously sized vents will make sure your Pi gets the air it needs to stay cool. A slot on the front wall makes it possible to let a GPIO cable through and thus extend and hack the system.

The Minitux case fits perfectly around our Crafty Pi Monster prototyping board, making it an ideal choice as a generic enclosure for building clean-looking custom projects.

The back of the case has two holes designed to fit the VESA 75 specification, making it easy to mount your Pi on the back of a monitor or small TV.

Like the original Tuxedo case, it has been quasi-scientifically proven to pass the 'Living Room Aesthetics Test' 99.99% of the time (a.k.a 'The Spouse Test') ;-)

Specifications

  • Clear acrylic
  • Assembly time: about five minutes
  • Access to all of Pi's connectors
  • Features vents on the bottom, top, left and right walls
  • Works with any Raspberry Pi with mounting holes, models A and B (won't work with a rev.1, please consider our Tuxedo case for rev.1 Pis)
  • Outer dimensions: 101mm x 70mm x 33.3mm
  • Inner dimensions: 86mm x 55mm x 27.6mm (may vary due to thickness variations on acrylic plates)
  • 3mm thick black acrylic case (7 pieces, including left walls for both models A and B)
  • 4 black screws
  • 4 hex aluminum standoffs
  • 4 rubber feet
  • 2 VESA 75 mounting holes
  • GPIO ribbon cable slot
  • Can accommodate heat sinks

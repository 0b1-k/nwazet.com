Nwazet USB Power Supply Kit


Reg. Price: $13.00
Now $3.00
Out of Stock - Product Discontinued

The Nwazet Power Supply module is the easiest way to power your project away from a computer once you're done building it.

This power module is equipped with a standard center-positive 2.0mm barrel jack and will take any power supply, such as a wall-wart, a 9 volt battery or a cigarette-lighter adapter, between 7 and 20 volts and will output a nicely regulated 5 volts at up to 800 mA.

Just connect the USB cable that you're using today to the power supply module and you're good to go.

The module also features a large switch to let you turn your project ON / OFF smoothly and easily.

Power Specifications

  • Voltage Input: 7v (min) to 20v (max)
  • Voltage Output: 5v regulated
  • Current Output: 800 mA (max)
  • 1.0v Dropout
  • Barrel Jack: 2.0mm diameter with the positive voltage at the center

Dimensions

  • The hole-to-hole dimensions of the module are 30mm x 25mm.
  • The total size of the board is 40mm x 35mm.

Schematics

  • Eagle schematics, BOM and datasheets.

Note: No USB cable or wall-wart is provided with the power supply module. This module is sold as a kit and requires soldering.

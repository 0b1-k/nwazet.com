Payment Policy

Accepted Methods of Payment

  • Credit Card (Visa, Master Card, American Express, Discover)
  • Check, cashier check, money Order, wire transfer: only for established resellers (please contact us); no personal checks are accepted

Credit Card Charges

Your credit card will be charged when you place your order. If we are not able to fulfill your order for any reason your credit card will be refunded.

Mailing Address For Check/Money Order

Please contact us for check or money orders.

Shipping Policy

Turnaround

All orders are shipped within 48 hours Monday - Friday 8am - 5pm, except for items on back order.

Carriers

We use the USPS to deliver orders. As requested, we can use FedEx, UPS or DHL on a case by case basis and for reseller orders.

Order Tracking

If a tracking # is provided by the shipping carrier, we will update your order with the tracking information. Please note that some orders using 1st Class USPS mail will not have tracking numbers.

Shipping Rates

The rate charged for the shipping of your order is based on the weight of your products, and your location. Before the final checkout page you will be shown what the cost of shipping will be, and you will have a chance to not place your order if you decide not to.

Back Orders

If an item goes on back order we will ship you the complete order once all the items are in stock. When the item becomes available we will ship the complete order. We will contact you if any part of your order is on back order and will give you the opportunity to cancel it or remove the unavailable items. You will be refunded for the whole order or for the cancelled items.

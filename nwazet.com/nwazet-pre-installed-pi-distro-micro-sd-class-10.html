Nwazet Pre-Installed Pi Distro - SanDisk Ultra 8 GB Class 10 Micro-SDHC

$15.00

Product Type: Operating Systems

Freedom of choice is a beautiful thing! With the Raspberry Pi, there are several different Operating Systems that you can choose from, each one with its own strengths and specialized features. It's like being able to pick the 'best tool for the job' when putting an application together or being able to learn new skills with something entirely new and exotic. Regardless of your goals, we'll make it fast and easy for you to get started with your Raspberry Pi by pre-installing and testing the latest version of your distribution of choice before shipping it to you.

For best performance and reliability, we have standardized on SanDisk Class 10 SDHC cards with a capacity of 8GB and ultra fast read/write speeds.

You can read more details about each Operating System on the Raspberry Pi download page.

  • Manufacturer: SanDisk
  • Class: 10
  • Capacity: 8GB
  • Form factor: micro-SDHC
  • Write speed: up to 10MB/second
  • Read speed: up to 30MB/second

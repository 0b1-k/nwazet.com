Raspberry Pi Infrared Camera

$30.00
Out of Stock - Product Discontinued

Product Type: Data Acquisition, Input Devices, Optical Solutions, Sensors

If the Raspberry Pi was a revolution in the way we design microcontroller projects and embedded systems, its camera has similar potential. This is no mere MJPEG, serial, or even USB camera like those that until now were in projects requiring photo or video capabilities. With its specialized CSI connector on the Raspberry Pi, and its uncompromising design, it redefines what it is possible to do on such an inexpensive setup.

The Raspberry Pi Camera can shoot 1080p30 true high definition video, as well as 5 megapixel static images.

It is fully supported by all recent distributions of Linux for the Raspberry Pi, making it easy to shoot photos and videos from the command-line, or from your own programs. Mobile applications exist that enable control of the camera from a phone. Standard Linux image processing libraries can be used to enable facial recognition, and other amazing applications.

Dimensions

  • 25mm x 20mm x 9mm

Specifications

  • Video resolutions: 1080p30, 720p60, 640x480p60, 640x480p90
  • Photo resolution: 2592 x 1944 (5 megapixels)
  • Pi NoIR: no IR filter, includes blue filter
  • CSI bus

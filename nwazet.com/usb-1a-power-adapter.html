5.5V DC 1A USB Power Adapter With Cable - UL listed

$7.00

Product Type: Power Solutions

This nice, small, lightweight, and efficient USB switching DC power supply is perfect to reliably power a Raspberry Pi, or any device that feeds on USB.

It is thin and fits in power strips without blocking other outlets. The output is regulated so you'll get a steady 5.5V DC up to 1A of current draw. The provided voltage is a little higher than 5V, to account for the voltage drop that usually happens in USB cords with greedy devices such as the Pi.

The provided 0.9m cord features a standard micro-USB. This supply is designed to work anywhere in the world on 100V-240V AC 50 or 60Hz wall power; you'll just need a low-cost plug adapter.

The red LED on top will let you know the adapter is powered. It works great for a variety of applications, micro-controllers and of course, the Raspberry Pi.

Dimensions

  • Wall wart dimension above the power plug: 59mm x 24.3mm x 46.3mm (2.3" x 0.95" x 1.8")
  • Micro-USB Cable length: 90cm (35")
  • Cable diameter: 3.5mm (0.13")

Specifications

  • Input voltage: 100V-240V AC (standard US plug, no ground)
  • Output voltage: 5.5 VDC @ 1A
  • Regulated, switched output
  • CE / UL listed
